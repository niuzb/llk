1.在build.xml 中增加如下两个target
<target name="enabletraffic_bj">
		<property name="customization" value="traffic_bj" />
		<property name="build.traffic_bj" value="true" />
	</target>
		
	<target name="buildtraffic_bj"
			description="customizes this project with the settings found in resources/traffic_bj"
			depends="enabletraffic_bj, j2mepolish"
	/>
	
2.在build.xml target  buildAllCustomizations 中增加一行。

3。在resource文件夹中增加一个对应城市的文件夹。
4。在model.java文件中增加一个函数initRoadHash，添加该城市的数据

5。编译即可
